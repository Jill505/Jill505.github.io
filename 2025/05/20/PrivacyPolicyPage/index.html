Privacy Policy

Falabbo game studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, play Crazy Ambulance, use our services, or interact with us in any way. Please read this policy carefully.

  1. Information We Collect
    We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, or other information you provide voluntarily.

Usage Data: including your IP address, browser type, access times, and the pages you view.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: to enhance user experience and analyze usage.

Account Information: such as your username, email address, and password when you create an account.

Gameplay Data: including your in-game progress, achievements, preferences, and interactions with other players in Crazy Ambulance.

Device Information: such as device ID, operating system, language settings, and mobile network.

Payment Information: if you make purchases, we may collect billing information (processed through third-party payment providers).

Analytics: we use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to better understand user behavior.

  1. How We Use Your Information
    We use the collected information to:

Provide, operate, and maintain our services, including Crazy Ambulance

Improve, personalize, and expand gameplay experience

Process in-game purchases and provide customer support

Communicate with you, including for service updates and marketing (with your consent)

Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activity

Comply with legal obligations

  1. How We Share Your Information
    We may share your information:

With trusted service providers that assist in operations such as cloud storage, analytics, and customer service

With advertising partners to show in-game ads (only with proper consent where required)

To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests

In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger, acquisition)

With your consent or as otherwise disclosed at the time of data collection

  1. Your Rights and Choices
    You may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including:

The right to access, update, or delete your personal information

The right to opt-out of marketing communications

The right to restrict or object to certain types of data processing

To exercise your rights, please contact us at orangec.org@gmail.com

  1. Data Retention
    We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

  2. Security
    We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

  3. Children’s Privacy
    Our services, including Crazy Ambulance, are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under the applicable age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

  4. Changes to This Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the “Effective Date” or through in-game or website notices.

  5. Contact Us
    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: orangec.org@gmail.com
